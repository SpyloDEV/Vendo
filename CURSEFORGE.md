# Vendo

Vendo is a lightweight and modern World of Warcraft addon that makes vendor interactions effortless.

Clean. Fast. Invisible.

---

## Features

- Automatic Repairs  
  Repairs your gear automatically when you open a vendor. Supports guild bank repairs if available and permitted.

- Automatic Gray Item Selling  
  Automatically sells all poor-quality (gray) items with accurate, stack-aware gold tracking.

- Clean Chat Summary  
  Minimal, color-coded chat messages showing exactly how much gold you spent or earned.

- In-Game Options Menu  
  Toggle features easily via the AddOns settings panel.

---

## Commands (optional)

/kt on – Enable Vendo  
/kt off – Disable Vendo  
/kt repair – Toggle automatic repairs  
/kt sell – Toggle gray item selling  
/kt chat – Toggle chat messages

---

## Why Vendo?

- No intrusive UI
- No feature bloat
- Minimal performance overhead
- Designed to “just work” in the background
